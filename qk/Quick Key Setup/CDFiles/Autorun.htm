Quick Key 5.1

 

You can use Quick Key 5.1 to insert foreign letters, symbols, and mathematical operators into any document. Quick Key 5.1 offers three different ways to accomplish this task: by keystroke sending, by drag and drop, and by copying the character to the clipboard. In addition, you can easily create and customize your own groups of characters; however, Quick Key 5.1 comes with a pre-installed package of over 90 different character sets.

 

To install Quick Key 5.1, please complete these steps. If prompted with the “File Download” box, click Open and follow instructions to complete setup.
