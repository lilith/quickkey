Introducing Quick Key 5.1

You can use Quick Key to insert foreign letters, symbols, and mathematical operators into any document. Quick Key offers three different ways to accomplish this task: by keystroke sending, by drag and drop, and by copying the character to the clipboard. In addition, you can easily create and customize your own groups of characters; however, Quick Key comes with a pre-installed package of over 200 different character sets.
